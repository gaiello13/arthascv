Arthas Menethil

The Lich King

Son of King Terenas Menethil II and former Prince of Lorderon. Culler of Stratholm, Weilder of Frostmourne, Leader of the Scourge.


Contact

Noteable "Accomplishments"

  1. The Culling of Stratholm
    • Resulted in the disbanding of The Knights of the Silver Hand.
    • Purged the city of corrupted townsfolk to free them from the Scourge.

  2. The Retrieval of the Cursed Runeblade Frostmourne
    • A powerful Runeblade that corrupts its user and consumes the souls of its victims.
    • Assisted Arthas in achieving the demise of the Dread Lord Mal'Ganis.

  3. Scourge Invasion of Azeroth
    • A calculated attack in attempts to lure powerful heroes to Northrend and select from the strongest as his new champions of the scourge.
    • Defeated during the final attack on Icecrown Citadel led by Tirion Fordring.
